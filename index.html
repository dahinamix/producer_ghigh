"Known for creating dreamy and sensory soundscapes — a distinctive style that the public keeps falling in love with."

G-high is a co-founder of MonoTree, the K-pop publishing and producing powerhouse he built alongside Hwang Hyun and Joohyoung Lee. Over more than a decade, the company has become a defining creative force in the Korean music industry.

His production credits span some of the genre's most iconic acts — LOONA, Ladies' Code, Red Velvet, Stellar, SEVENTEEN, tripleS, ARTMS, and many more. Each project carries a signature quality: lush, layered atmospheres that feel simultaneously intimate and cinematic.

Where other producers chase trends, G-high builds worlds. His music invites listeners in and doesn't let go — a sensory experience as much as a sonic one.
